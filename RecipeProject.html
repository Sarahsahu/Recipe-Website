GULAB JAMUN

Ingrediants

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Make dough using milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk.
  2. Boil sugar syrup with cardamom and rose water.
  3. Roll smooth balls from the dough.
  4. Fry on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Soak in warm syrup for at least 1 hour.

BARFI

Ingrediants

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix bread with condensed milk and ghee.
  2. Cook till thick and remove from heat.
  3. Spread in a greased tray.
  4. Garnish with nuts and let it set.
  5. Cut into squares after cooling.
